About Me

I am an aspiring web developer with a strong foundation in web development and nearly a decade of experience in hospitality, customer service, and retail, particularly as a Pharmacy Assistant Supervisor. My recent completion of a Certificate in Applied Software Development at Dev Academy equipped me with essential skills in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, TypeScript, React, Node.js, Express.js, SQLite3, and RESTful APIs.

My passion for web development ignited during a university project where I created a website, inspiring me to pursue a career dedicated to building meaningful, user-centered digital solutions. Currently, I am actively developing personal projects, including an e-commerce site and a budgeting app, where I apply my problem-solving abilities and manage complex tasks.

I thrive in collaborative environments that promote continuous learning, and I bring strong leadership, multitasking, and teamwork skills from my previous roles. I am driven by a desire for flexibility and a future-proof career that balances professional aspirations with family life.

I am eager to connect with like-minded professionals and explore opportunities for a Junior Web Developer position where I can contribute my technical skills, adaptability, and passion for innovation to a dynamic team.
